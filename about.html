Our Story

About OM Juice & Shakes

How a quiet obsession became a craft juice & shake counter in Rewari.

The Pour

OM Juice & Shakes began as a quiet obsession — the conviction that a juice or shake could carry the same craft as a fine cocktail or a plate from a three-star kitchen.

Every ingredient is sourced for a reason. Every technique — from the slow infusion of cream to the tableside pour — is designed for one thing: to make you stop, taste, and remember.

We don't batch. We don't pre-mix. Each shake is built to order, pulled by hand, and poured in front of you. Because the best part of a shake isn't just drinking it — it's watching it come together.
